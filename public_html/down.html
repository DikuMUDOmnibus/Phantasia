Welcome to Phantasia 4, a free, kick butt, online, multi-player, dungeons and dragons, fantasy role-playing, adventure game. All characters currently online can adventure together in this dungeons and dragons style realm. Become a Magic-user, Fighter, Elf, Dwarf or Halfling and fight evil throughout the realm, beat on your friends, pillage the countryside, horde gold and rescue helpless virgins.
